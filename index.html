About

Hi, I'm Tahnee. I teach ceramics for people who don't take themselves seriously.

High Low Ceramics is my approach to teaching: rigorous enough to actually help you improve, loose enough to remember why you started. I've been teaching wheel throwing and handbuilding for over a decade, and I bring a background in dance and athletics to every class.

That means you'll hear a lot about body mechanics, movement, and injury prevention alongside the actual clay stuff. Because you can't throw a pot if your back's out.
